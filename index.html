Our Popular Dishes

Discover our most beloved dishes, crafted with authentic spices and traditional recipes passed down through generations. Each plate tells a story of Indian culinary excellence.

Channa Masala

A classic North Indian dish featuring tender chickpeas simmered in a rich, spiced tomato gravy with onions, ginger and aromatic spices. Perfectly paired with rice or naan.

Chicken Tikka

Tender pieces of chicken marinated in yogurt and spices, then grilled to perfection in our tandoor. Served with mint chutney and onion rings for an authentic taste experience.

Butter Chicken

Our signature dish featuring tender tandoori chicken in a rich, creamy tomato sauce with butter and aromatic spices. A beloved classic that keeps our customers coming back.

Tandoori Chicken

Whole chicken marinated in yogurt and traditional spices, then slow-cooked in our clay tandoor oven. Served with onions and lemon for an authentic tandoori experience.

Curry Pakora

Crispy vegetable fritters made with chickpea flour and Indian spices, served in a flavorful curry sauce. A perfect combination of crispy and saucy textures.

Gulab Jamun

Traditional Indian dessert of soft milk-solid dumplings, fried until golden and soaked in aromatic sugar syrup flavored with cardamom and rose water. A sweet ending to any meal.
